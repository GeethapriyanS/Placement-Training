Sorting Algorithm & Stability
Selection Sort → ❌ Unstable

Swaps distant elements, disrupting the order of duplicates.
Bubble Sort → ✅ Stable

Uses adjacent swaps, keeping equal elements in order.
Merge Sort → ✅ Stable

Maintains order while merging sorted subarrays.
Insertion Sort → ✅ Stable

Inserts elements without disturbing previous duplicates.
Quick Sort → ❌ Unstable

Swaps and partitioning may change the order of duplicates.
